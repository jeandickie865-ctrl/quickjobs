The ShiftMatch application is a German-language, two-sided mobile marketplace connecting freelance/employed workers (Worker) with employers (Employer). The primary goal is to build a polished, stable, and performant app with a modern dark theme (BACKUP) and a native iOS feel.

**Core User Requirements:**
1.  **Worker & Employer Flows:** Users must be able to register, manage their profiles, and interact with the job lifecycle.
2.  **Job Matching:** Workers should see a feed of relevant jobs based on their profile (skills, location, radius). This matching must be reliable.
3.  **UI/UX Excellence:** The application must be visually consistent and handle device-specific UI challenges like the iPhone's safe areas and keyboard. All interactions should be smooth and intuitive.
4.  **Performance:** List screens, particularly Matches, must load quickly, even with a large number of entries.
5.  **Deployment:** The application must be buildable for iOS (TestFlight) and Android, requiring correct configuration of , , and all related dependencies.

- Numerous UI/UX refinements have been made (toasts, button states, layout fixes).
- Critical bugs related to data mapping (/), authentication, and job matching have been resolved.

- A successful iOS build was produced after an extensive debugging cycle.
